A paragraph is a self-contained unit of discourse in writing that deals with a particular point or idea. It is characterized by the unity and coherence of ideas among sentences, rather than just its length. Typically, a paragraph consists of at least one sentence and is often designated by an indent or a line break, serving to express a speaker's thoughts clearly on a specific topic.
